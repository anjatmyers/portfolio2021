About Me

My name is Andrea and I am a full-stack software engineer student. I am currently enrolled in the DigitalCrafts Coding Bootcamp and am set to graduate March 2021.
I graduated from the University of Virginia in 2017 with a degree in public health. However, in 2020 I decided to make a transition from the medical field to software engineering. I love working with computers and find it so satisfying to build something from the ground up. Premed course work and patient exposure prepped me for a different career path but the skills I learned along the way are well suited to software developemnt. In previous experiences it was critical to develop a sharp attention to detail, strong work ethic, the ability to listen to the needs of others and display clear understanding, and many more that come from working in service of others. I'm excited to continue my growth as a developer in hopes that I can make technology more functional and accessible to the people that need it the most.
